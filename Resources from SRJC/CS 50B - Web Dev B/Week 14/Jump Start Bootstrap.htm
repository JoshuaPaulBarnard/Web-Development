Welcome to the blog of NorCal Traveler!

Here you will find articles and videos with advice and tips for traveling, along with photos and videos from around The World! I was born and raised in the cold, foggy, mountainous climate of Northern California, and from here I have started my adventures around the world!  And this is where my name comes from, NorCal meaning Northern California, and Traveler as I am a World Traveler having visited 22 countries, so far!
